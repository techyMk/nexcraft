## About NexCart

NexCart is an AI-native ecommerce platform built for the next generation of
digital shopping. We curate flagship technology — smartphones, laptops,
audio gear, gaming, wearables, smart-home devices and AI gadgets — and
deliver them faster, smarter, and with better service than any traditional
retailer.

- **Tagline:** *The Future of Intelligent Commerce.*
- **Founded:** 2024
- **Headquarters:** Online-first, with fulfilment centres in the United
  States, the United Kingdom, the United Arab Emirates, India and
  Singapore.
- **Active in:** 120+ countries.
- **Customers served:** 50,000+ verified shoppers.
- **Average rating:** 4.9 out of 5.

NexCart is not a marketplace. We hand-pick every product we sell, work
directly with authorised brand distributors, and stand behind each item
with a 2-year warranty.

## Mission

We exist to make buying technology feel effortless. Every interaction —
search, recommendation, checkout, support — should be a 9-out-of-10
experience that respects the customer's time, taste and money.

Our north-star metric is **time from interest to delivery**. Today the
global median is 47 minutes for express markets and 2–3 business days for
standard delivery anywhere we serve.

## How NexCart is different

1. **AI-curated catalog.** Every product is scored, ranked and recommended
   by NexCart Intelligence™ — our proprietary recommendation system that
   blends taste, price sensitivity, and live demand signals.
2. **One trustworthy SKU per category.** We refuse to flood pages with
   near-identical listings. If we sell it, we believe it's the best in its
   price band.
3. **No marketplace sellers.** Every order ships from a NexCart fulfilment
   centre. No grey-market middlemen, no counterfeits.
4. **2-year warranty as standard.** Every product. Every category. No
   upselling protection plans at checkout.
5. **Lightning support.** Median first response: 38 seconds via the AI
   assistant, 4 minutes for a human handover.

## Pricing & money

### Currency

- Prices are displayed and charged in **US Dollars (USD)** by default.
- The storefront detects your region from the IP and shows
  reference-only local-currency conversions on every product page (e.g.
  "≈ ₹1,04,500").
- Your bank converts USD → local currency at the rate effective on the
  day the charge posts. A foreign-transaction fee may apply depending on
  your card issuer (typically 0–3%).
- VAT / GST is calculated at checkout based on the shipping address and
  shown as a separate line before the **Place order** button.

### Tax

## Returns & Refunds

### 7-day return window

Every item ships with a **7-day return window**, starting from the date of
delivery. No questions asked. The product must be:

- In its original packaging
- With all accessories included
- Not engraved, customised or personalised
- Without physical damage caused by misuse

### How to return

1. Go to **Account → Orders → [order number] → Return this item**
2. Pick a reason (we use this to improve, not to deny returns)
3. Print the prepaid return label OR schedule a free pickup
4. Drop off at any carrier location or hand to the pickup courier

### Refund timing

- Refunds are issued to the original payment method within **3–5 business
  days** of us receiving the returned item.
- For Stripe-processed card payments, your bank may take an additional
  2–3 business days to post the credit.
- Store credit refunds are instant.

### Non-returnable items

- Software, in-app purchases, and digital content once activated
- Items missing the original packaging or accessories
- Personalised or engraved products

### Exchanges

We don't process direct exchanges — easier to refund the returned item and
place a new order for the replacement. Both happen in parallel so you're
never waiting.

## Warranty

Every product NexCart sells carries a **2-year limited warranty** against
manufacturing defects, regardless of what the manufacturer offers
elsewhere. This is in addition to any rights you have under local consumer
law.

The warranty covers:
- Hardware defects not caused by accident, misuse or unauthorised repair
- Battery capacity dropping below 80% within the warranty period
- Display, port, and component failures under normal use

The warranty does **not** cover:
- Accidental damage (drops, liquid, electrical surges)
- Cosmetic wear and tear
- Software-only issues unrelated to hardware

To claim: **Account → Orders → [order number] → Open a warranty claim**, or
email warranty@nexcart.app. We aim to triage within 24 hours and ship a
replacement or repair within 5–7 business days.

For accidental damage, **NexCart Protect+** is an optional add-on at
checkout — covers drops, spills and surge damage for 2 years.

## Common questions

**Do I need an account to shop?**
No. Guest checkout works. An account unlocks order history, wishlist sync,
loyalty tiers, and faster repeat checkouts.

**Is my Google sign-in tied to my email account?**
Yes — your NexCart profile is linked to the Google email you signed in
with. If you later sign in with email + password using the same email,
both methods will work on the same account.

**Can I change the email on my account?**
Yes, from Account → Settings → Email. We'll send a verification code to
both the old and the new address to confirm.

**Do you price-match?**
We monitor competitor pricing daily and aim to be within 1% of the best
authorised-retailer price. If you find a verifiable lower price on the
same SKU from an authorised seller, email match@nexcart.app within 14
days of purchase and we'll credit you the difference.

**How do AI Picks work?**
"AI Pick" appears on products our recommendation engine ranks in the top
5% of their category and price band, based on quality signals, customer
ratings, and how often the product is genuinely loved (not just
purchased).

**What if my product arrives damaged?**
Open a return within 48 hours under **Account → Orders → Report damage**.
We'll either replace the unit at no cost or issue a full refund — your
choice. No need to ship the damaged item back; we'll usually ask for a
photo.

**Can I buy NexCart gift cards?**
Yes — denominations from $25 to $500, delivered by email. Available under
**Shop → Gift cards** (rolling out region by region).

**How do I become a NexCart admin or seller?**
We don't have a marketplace, so there's no public seller programme.
For brand partnership and supply enquiries, email
partnerships@nexcart.app.

**What payment data does NexCart store?**
None of your card numbers. Stripe stores a token we can charge with your
permission. We can see only the last four digits, expiry month/year, and
card brand.

**Can I delete my account?**
Yes — Account → Settings → Privacy → Delete account. Active orders must
be complete or cancelled first. Deletion is irreversible.
